Overview

This demonstration showcases the comprehensive vintage forecasting system with FICO segmentation and flexible data handling capabilities. The system combines vintage analysis (loan performance by origination period) with FICO segmentation and seasoning curves to create accurate forecasts for future charge-offs.

This is particularly valuable for long-term loans with terms exceeding 10 years and portfolios with varying credit quality characteristics.
